Building Memories Together: Interactive Wedding Website Project

June 6, 2025 Oiyan c.

Recently, I had the wonderful opportunity to create a dedicated website for our wedding. My main goal was to make the experience as seamless and enjoyable as possible for all our guests. To achieve this, I designed a user-friendly RSVP form that allowed everyone to confirm their attendance online with just a few clicks.

On the site, guests could easily access the detailed wedding day timetable, which provided a clear overview of all the activities planned throughout our celebration. This helped everyone know what to expect and when to join in on the special moments.

Now that the wedding has taken place, I’ve updated the website with a collection of beautiful photos and a lively SDE (Same Day Edit) video. This allows our guests to relive the highlights and cherish the memories we created together on that memorable day.

Overall, this project has been a meaningful way to keep our loved ones connected, informed, and involved—both before and after our wedding—and to share our joy with everyone who celebrated with us.
